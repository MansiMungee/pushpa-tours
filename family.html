Wildlife Safaris for Families

Kaziranga is one of the most rewarding family wildlife experiences in India. Jeep safaris, rhinos, elephants, grasslands, wetlands and birds make it exciting for children and adults alike.

Lakes, Gardens & Gentle Walks

Enjoy calm days around Umiam Lake, flower gardens, parks, waterfalls, viewpoints and short scenic walks that work well for mixed-age families.

Monasteries & Mountain Towns

Tawang, Gangtok, Namchi and nearby Himalayan towns offer monasteries, mountain views, prayer flags, cable cars, gardens and peaceful cultural experiences.

Museums, Culture & Learning

Add child-friendly learning experiences through museums, local markets, craft traditions, village visits, tea gardens and cultural centres.

Homestays & Local Food

Stay in welcoming family-run homestays, enjoy home-cooked meals and experience the warmth of local hospitality without sacrificing basic comfort and safety.

Soft Adventure

Gentle hikes, village walks, boating, cable cars, picnic spots and nature trails can be added for families who want light adventure without a physically demanding itinerary.

Family-friendly destinations we can include

These destinations can be visited individually or combined into a larger family holiday, depending on your time, interests and comfort preferences.

Assam

Kaziranga National Park

A classic family wildlife destination with jeep safaris, rhinos, elephants, wetlands, grasslands and rich birdlife. Kaziranga works beautifully for children, first-time wildlife travellers and families who want a safe, exciting introduction to Northeast India’s wild side.

Meghalaya

Shillong, Umiam Lake & Cherrapunji

Shillong and Cherrapunji offer an easy family mix of lakes, waterfalls, caves, parks, viewpoints, museums, cafes and living-root-bridge landscapes. These routes are ideal for families who want nature, comfort and short scenic outings.

Arunachal Pradesh

Tawang, Dirang & Ziro Valley

For families who love mountain roads and culture, western Arunachal offers monasteries, snow views, valleys, village landscapes and homestays. Ziro Valley adds a gentler cultural experience with paddy fields, Apatani villages, traditional homes and quiet countryside.

Sikkim

Gangtok & Namchi

Gangtok and Namchi are excellent for families who want clean mountain towns, monasteries, flower gardens, cable cars, viewpoints and easy-paced sightseeing. These destinations work especially well for elders and children when planned with enough rest time.

Assam

Majuli, Tea Gardens & River Culture

Majuli and Assam’s tea landscapes offer a slower cultural holiday with boat rides, village life, local food, monasteries, artisans, handloom traditions and relaxed stays in eco-lodges or heritage-style accommodations.

Nagaland

Mokokchung, Kohima & Naga Heritage

Families interested in culture can explore Naga heritage, village life, food, craft traditions and festivals. These journeys are best planned slowly, with local guidance and comfortable stays.
