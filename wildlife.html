Charismatic Mammal Safaris

Explore Kaziranga’s iconic floodplain landscape for Greater One-horned Rhinoceros, Asian Elephant, Wild Water Buffalo, deer, raptors and, with luck, Bengal Tiger. Ideal for first-time wildlife travellers, families and photographers.

Elusive Mammals of Cloud Forests

Eaglenest and the surrounding Eastern Himalayan forests are suited to patient naturalists interested in harder-to-see species such as red panda, small cats, porcupines, civets, squirrels, primates and other secretive mammals.

Herpetofauna & Night Walks

Slow forest walks after rain can reveal frogs, snakes, lizards and other nocturnal life of the Eastern Himalayan cloud forests. These tours are ideal for naturalists, photographers and travellers who enjoy careful observation.

Butterflies, Dragonflies & Beetles

For macro-photography enthusiasts, the region offers extraordinary smaller wildlife: butterflies, dragonflies, jewel beetles, moths, spiders and many other forest invertebrates that are often missed on conventional wildlife tours.

Fungi, Orchids & Hidden Forest Worlds

The wet forests of Northeast India are alive with seasonal fungi, orchids, mosses and glowing forest details. Specialist walks can focus on bioluminescent fungi, forest ecology, plant life and close-up photography.

Family-friendly Wildlife Journeys

We also design easier wildlife holidays with comfortable stays, jeep safaris, river landscapes, short walks, cultural stops and flexible pacing for mixed-age groups and first-time Northeast India visitors.

Signature wildlife landscapes

Our wildlife routes focus on the same broad Northeast India circuit as our birding tours, but each itinerary is tuned to the wildlife groups, seasons and experiences you care about most.

Kaziranga

Grassland safaris and iconic mammals

Kaziranga is the classic big-wildlife experience of Assam: tall elephant grass, wetlands, woodland patches and open floodplains shaped by the Brahmaputra. This is the place for rhinos, elephants, wild buffalo, deer, raptors and jeep safaris through one of India’s most dramatic wildlife landscapes.

Eaglenest

Cloud forests, small mammals and nocturnal life

Eaglenest offers a completely different wildlife experience: misty forests, mountain roads, deep valleys, night sounds and subtle encounters. It is best suited to travellers who enjoy slow natural history — looking for mammals, frogs, snakes, insects, fungi, orchids and the small details that make cloud forests unforgettable.

Nameri & Pakke landscape

River forests, elephants and quieter wilderness

Nameri combines semi-evergreen forest, riverine habitats and the Jia Bhoreli landscape. It is a quieter complement to Kaziranga and works beautifully for travellers interested in forest walks, river landscapes, elephants, birds, butterflies and relaxed nature-based travel.
